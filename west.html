Bundu Mask

ID

  • 19th to 20th century
  • Wood, cloth, and fiber
  • Mende peoples
  • Sierra Leone
  • Sande Society

History

  • Worn on top of head
  • Women wore mask and men made it
  • One of the few masks women wore
  • Ideal woman (think venus of willendorf)
  • Created to help girls go through initiation bundu society and teach them how to be good wives and mothers

Features

  • Scarification marks
  • Small eyes and mouth to represent the lack of gossip
  • Neck rings symbolizes fertility and good health
